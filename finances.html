Navigating Scholarships and Financial Aid

Taylor Marks | November 1, 2017 | 3:00pm | Venice, Italy

Study abroad is a great experience that everyone should be able to take advantage of. However, it also costs a lot of money. Between the astronomical costs of flights, living expenses, food, tuition, school supplies, travel supplies, insurance, and a whole host of other things, the money adds up. Most students lack the funds up front to pay these bills. But, luckily, there is still hope. Many programs offer substantial scholarships to students, usually based on merit or financial need.
